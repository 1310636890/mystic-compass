## Tips for Promoting Feng Shui Affiliate Programs

### 1. Content Types That Convert

| Content Type | Example | Best For |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| Product reviews | "Feng Shui World Review" | Product stores |
| Comparison posts | "Joey Yap vs Karen Rauch Carter" | Courses |
| How-to guides | "How to Choose a Luo Pan Compass" | Products |
| Best-of lists | "Best Feng Shui Crystals for Wealth" | Products |
| Case studies | "How I Feng Shui'd My Home Office" | Consulting |

### 2. Traffic Sources That Work

- **SEO** — "feng shui products review", "best feng shui courses", "feng shui compass store" — good search volume, low competition
- **Pinterest** — Feng Shui visuals perform extremely well (room layouts, crystal guides, Bagua maps)
- **YouTube** — Room tours, desk setup guides, product unboxings
- **Reddit** — r/FengShui, r/InteriorDesign, r/malelivingspace, r/femalelivingspace

### 3. Things to Avoid

- Don't bid on brand names in PPC unless the program explicitly allows it (most don't)
- Don't make health or financial claims ("this crystal will make you rich") — compliance risk
- Don't spam — Feng Shui audiences are savvy and will call you out
