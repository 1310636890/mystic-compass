## Top Feng Shui Courses Ranked

### 1. Joey Yap's Feng Shui Mastery Program
- **Best for**: Aspiring professional consultants
- **Format**: Online modules + live Q&A
- **Covers**: Classical Feng Shui, BaZi, Flying Stars, Eight Mansions, date selection
- **Price range**: $500-$3,000 depending on level
- **Why we recommend**: Joey Yap is the most recognized Feng Shui educator globally. His courses are structured, comprehensive, and respected in the industry.

### 2. Karen Rauch Carter's Feng Shui School
- **Best for**: Interior designers and home enthusiasts
- **Format**: Self-paced video courses
- **Covers**: Western Bagua Feng Shui, clutter clearing, space energetics
- **Price range**: $200-$1,000
- **Why we recommend**: Practical, modern approach. No Chinese metaphysics knowledge required. Perfect for applying Feng Shui to Western homes.

### 3. Feng Shui Master Consultant Course
- **Best for**: Budget-conscious learners wanting certification
- **Format**: Downloadable materials + online tests
- **Covers**: Complete Feng Shui curriculum
- **Price range**: $100-$400
- **Why we recommend**: Most affordable path to a Feng Shui certification. Good foundation for starting a consulting practice.

### 4. Free Options: YouTube & Udemy
- **Feng Shui Tips YouTube channels** — Search "Feng Shui basics" on YouTube. Start with channels by Joey Yap, Anjie Cho, or Dear Modern.
- **Udemy Feng Shui courses** — Frequently on sale for $10-$20. Decent introductions but not comprehensive.
