## In-Person vs Online Feng Shui Consultation — Which Is Better?

| Type | Pros | Cons | Price |
|------|------|------|-------|
| **In-Person** | Most accurate, walk-through, real-time adjustments | Expensive, travel fees, schedule conflicts | $300–1,500 |
| **Distance/Online** | Affordable, no travel, faster turnaround | Less precise, relies on photos/floor plans | $100–500 |

> 💡 **Tip:** Start with a distance consultation first. If you like the consultant's approach, upgrade to in-person. Don't drop $1,500 on someone you've never spoken to.

## Questions to Ask Before Hiring a Feng Shui Consultant

1. What school/lineage do they follow? (Classical, Black Hat, Compass, etc.)
2. Do they have before/after case studies?
3. Will they sell you products after the consultation? (red flag if they push hard)
4. What's included in the fee? (Written report? Follow-up call? Floor plan analysis?)
5. How long have they been practicing?
