## The Bottom Line

Feng Shui Online is a German-based consulting platform. If your audience is in Europe, this is the most convenient option — consultations happen in European timezones, in English and German, starting at $100 per session.

At 20% commission, a $250 consultation (their average) nets you $50. Not life-changing, but consistent — consultation clients tend to book multiple sessions, not just one.

Here's the thing about promoting consulting services: the conversion rate is low (maybe 1 in 200 visitors books a paid consultation), but the per-sale value is high enough ($50-100/sale) that it still works if your traffic is targeted.

## What Actually Happens in a Consultation

Feng Shui Online offers four service types:

1. **Residential Consultation** — Your home analyzed room by room. Floor plan + photos + compass reading. They'll tell you where to put your bed, what color to paint the wealth corner, and which clutter is actively hurting you.
2. **Business Consultation** — Office layout, entrance positioning, desk arrangement for productivity and client attraction.
3. **Distance Analysis** — You send photos and floor plans, they send back a written report. Works for anyone anywhere on the planet.
4. **Coaching Packages** — Ongoing guidance, usually 3-6 sessions over several months. Higher lifetime value.

The distance analysis is the easiest sell for affiliates — no timezone coordination needed, lower price point ($100-150), faster turnaround (3-5 business days).

## The Affiliate Program

Direct program with auto-approval. 30-day cookie. $50 minimum payout. Free promotional materials provided — banners, email copy, and consultation description templates.

One important detail: the cookie is triggered on click, but the consultation booking process takes several steps (form → schedule → payment). If someone clicks your link, spends 3 days thinking about it, then books directly without clicking again, you still get credited within the 30-day window.

## Who Should Use This Platform

**✅ Good for:**
- Affiliates with European traffic (UK, Germany, Netherlands, Scandinavia)
- Content about "home office Feng Shui," "apartment layout," "moving into a new house"
- Sites that can build trust with long-form case studies and before/after content
- Anyone who wants auto-approve (no waiting for affiliate application review)

**❌ Skip if:**
- Your audience is price-sensitive (consultations aren't cheap)
- You need volume (consultations convert at lower rates than products)
- Your traffic is mostly US-based (Feng Shui Advantage is a better fit)

Feng Shui Online's distance analysis is perfect for this — the reader can do it from anywhere, no travel required.
