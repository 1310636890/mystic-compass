## The Bottom Line

Buy-FengShui.com fills the gap that Feng Shui World leaves open: US customers who want fast shipping and a familiar shopping experience. US-based warehouse means delivery in 3-5 days, not 7-14 from Singapore.

12% commission is modest, but here's the trade-off: the site is in proper English, product photos are clear, and the checkout process is standard US ecommerce. For Western audiences, the conversion rate will be higher than Feng Shui World's 2004-looking site, partially offsetting the lower commission.

## The Catalog

200+ products — smaller than Feng Shui World but more curated. Focus on popular items: crystals, fountains, wind chimes, Bagua mirrors. Less of the niche traditional tools that only practitioners buy. If your audience wants "Feng Shui home decor" not "professional Luo Pan compass," this is the better fit.

The crystal selection is decent. Prices are mid-range. One nice feature: product descriptions are written for people who don't already know Feng Shui terminology, which reduces bounce rate from search traffic.

## The Affiliate Program

In-house program. Manual approval (usually 24-48 hours). 30-day cookie. $50 minimum payout. Nothing special on program terms, but the US logistics are the selling point.

Average order value is around $60-70. At 12%, that's $7-8 per sale. You'd need about 7 sales to hit the $50 payout threshold — achievable if you're getting any meaningful traffic.
