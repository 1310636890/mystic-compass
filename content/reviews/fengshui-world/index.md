## The Bottom Line

I spent two hours clicking through Feng Shui World's entire catalog. Here's what nobody tells you: this is basically the Amazon of Feng Shui products. 500+ items. Everything from $5 incense to $500 professional Luo Pan compasses. And the 35% commission? It's real — highest in the entire niche.

But there's a catch. The website looks like 2004. Badly translated English. Some product photos are blurry. If your audience is Westerners who expect a polished Shopify experience, you'll lose them at the homepage. If your audience is Asian or already familiar with Feng Shui, they won't care.

**The numbers that matter:** Average order is $80. At 35%, that's $28 per sale. Free shipping kicks in at $150, which is a natural upsell point. With a 30-day cookie, you're earning on anything they buy for a month — not just what they clicked on.

## What It's Actually Like to Use

The product categories are Feng Shui World's biggest strength. Compasses (Luo Pan), crystals, Bagua mirrors, wind chimes, Buddha statues, wealth ships, Pi Yao figurines, bracelets, incense — they've got all the staples and then some.

Shipping is from Singapore. Delivery to the US takes about 7-14 days. Europe about the same. Australia and Asia faster. International shipping isn't cheap, but the $150 free shipping threshold is achievable for anyone buying a compass + a few crystals.

One thing I noticed: their "Feng Shui Bracelets & Jewelry" section converts way better than traditional tools. People browsing for jewelry buy 3x faster than people browsing for compasses. If you're building content, target jewelry and crystals — not just the hardcore practitioner stuff.

## The Affiliate Program

Feng Shui World runs their own direct affiliate program (not ShareASale or CJ). Application is instant — no approval wait. $50 minimum payout via PayPal or bank transfer.

The 35% rate applies to everything. Some programs tier by product category — Feng Shui World doesn't. Crystals, compasses, statues, all 35%.

One warning: their affiliate dashboard is basic. You get clicks, sales, and commission tracking — that's it. No deep linking tool, no product feeds, no banner generator. You'll need to pull product images manually for your content.
